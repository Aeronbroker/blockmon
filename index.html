blockmon by blockmon

A Modular System for Flexible, High-Performance Traffic Monitoring and Analysis

Blockmon is a modular system for high-performance traffic monitoring and analysis. Blockmon combines the flexibility that allows for a wide range of traffic monitoring and data analysis with the necessary mechanisms to yield high performance. In particular, Blockmon is able to capture up to 12 million minimum-sized packets per second on a 10Gb link on commodity hardware, and at line-rate for 128-byte packets and larger. Project website: http://fp7-demons.eu/
